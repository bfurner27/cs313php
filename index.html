About Me

My name is Benjamin Furner. I am from Mapleton Utah, a small town south of Provo. I come from an awesome family, I have two older sisters and three younger brothers. I currently study computer science at Brigham Young University Idaho in Rexburg. I love music, reading, eating, and doing fun things with my family. I play the piano and French Horn. I served a Haitian Creole speaking mission in Florida, Fort Lauderdale for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter day saints. I love helping people find happiness and peace through the gospel of Jesus Christ.
